Bulk Ordering

When properly stored, bulk foods have a long shelf life, some can even last forever. Common bulk foods include corn meal, corn, dry beans, white rice, wheat, powdered milk and pasta. A lot of people prefer bulk foods as it can be a DIY method of storage. Other items include baking powder, coffee, cocoa, tea, sugar, vinegar, honey and salt. A huge drawback to bulk foods when it comes to food storage is that it requires more creative cooking and preparation to create a variety of meals.
